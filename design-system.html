Elegant Design System

A sophisticated color palette with warm tones, perfect readability, and seamless light/dark mode transitions.

Color Philosophy

This design system uses warm, earthy tones that feel sophisticated yet approachable. The off-white base (#FFF8EC) provides a soft, elegant foundation that's easier on the eyes than pure white.

The dark mode counterpart uses warm dark browns instead of harsh blacks, maintaining the same elegant feel while ensuring excellent readability.

Typography & Spacing

Headings use slightly rounded fonts with careful letter-spacing for a modern, readable appearance. The type scale ensures clear hierarchy while maintaining visual harmony.

  • Subtle border radius (8-16px)
  • Soft shadows for depth
  • Accessible contrast ratios
  • Smooth transitions

Design Principles

🎨 Color Harmony

Warm, earthy tones create a sophisticated and welcoming atmosphere. Colors are carefully chosen to work together in both light and dark modes.

👁️ Accessibility First

All color combinations meet WCAG AA standards for contrast. Text remains highly readable in all lighting conditions.

✨ Subtle Interactions

Hover states and transitions provide feedback without being distracting. Shadows and elevation create natural depth.

📱 Responsive Design

The system adapts beautifully across devices while maintaining visual consistency and usability.
